HHHI IGUYS ITS ME TONGE!!!!!! (June, 29, 2026)

it is currently like...some ungodly hour of the night (it's only 12:25...am, the number sounds familiar...1225?) and i have spent a very large amount of time trying to make a little blue bunny guy walk around.

i am happy to report that the little bunny guy now walks around.

however there was this EVIL CURSE where every time i walked diagonally the camera would start having a nervous breakdown and jitter everywhere. i rewrote the movement code SO MANY TIMES that i started numbering the versions of it. i think i got to version 45.

version 45 won. YES ITS GOATED!!!

i have ERADICATED the diagonal jitter. hopefully forever, most likely forever. if it comes back i am moving into the woods broken heart emoji.

i also worked on the first area of the game some more!!! its called Lumenveil District now which sounds pretty and mysterious and lonely which is EXACTLY what i wanted. BUT THATS ALL I'M REVEALING FOR THIS PART TONIGHT!

AND!!! i finished the music for it!!! i cannot stop listening to it because every time i make a song for this game i immediately put it on loop for like an hour. now i'm not sure its good

but it does really set the feel for the subregion, its good in that way. and for that i am proud of it.

making games seems to be this process for today:

1. write code

4. suddenly know the answer

very magical process!!!! thrilling at certain times

okay goodbye for now i think i need sleep. (thats a lie i know damn well im gonna be up doing more bs for the game) BUT I'LL UPDATE YOU!
